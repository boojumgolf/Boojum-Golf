A Most Coveted Invitation

It is with great pleasure that we extend this highly coveted invitation to join us on Monday, July 13th, 2026, at the storied Springdale Golf Course — 200 Golfwatch Rd, Canton, NC 28716 — for the inaugural playing of what we fully intend to become a lasting and distinguished tradition.

While the championship itself will be contested with appropriate seriousness, The Boojum Open has always been conceived as something greater than a tournament. It is a celebration of enduring friendship. Ours is a fellowship built over years of shared experience, shared stories, and shared rounds. Golf may provide the framework for the day, but camaraderie gives it meaning.

The laughter between shots, the conversations on the tee box, and the gathering after the final putt are every bit as important as what appears on the scorecard. This first chapter sets the tone for many to come.

Expected Expenditures: Participants should anticipate covering greens fees (~$100), as well as lunch and beverages throughout the day's festivities. Final figures will be confirmed as we approach July. Additionally, should there be sufficient interest, we will explore coordinating group lodging — Airbnb, hotels, or camping — so that the fellowship may continue well beyond the 18th green and properly christen the beginning of this tradition.

Kindly confirm your attendance at your earliest convenience. Founding participants will forever hold a special distinction in the history of The Boojum Open.
